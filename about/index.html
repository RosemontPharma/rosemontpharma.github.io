Our Values

Our core values are driven by a desire to improve quality of life, achieve scientific excellence, operate with the highest standards of integrity and leadership, expand access to our products and employ a diverse workforce that values collaboration.

1. Patient Services:
Rosemont Pharma believes in its responsibility towards patients and satisfied their needs, therefore, it is committed to meet their needs and provide the right medicine at right time.

2. Customer Focus:
We are deeply committed to meeting the needs of our customers, and we constantly focus on customer satisfaction.

3. Employee Empowerment & Development:
Rosemont Pharma acknowledges its employees and their role in accomplishing its goals and in its growth, therefore, it is committed to recruit and keep individuals with high qualification, and it is committed to motivate and train its staff to improve their knowledge, skills, and abilities and empower them to provide excellent services to its customers. In return, it expects employees to perform effectively with outstanding professionalism, and loyalty.

4. Quality:
Quality is ingrained in the work of our colleagues and all our Values. We are dedicated to the delivery of high quality health care around the Middle East. Our business practices and processes are designed to achieve quality results that exceed the expectations of all our stakeholders.

5. Collaboration:
By championing high performing teams with diversity and inclusion.

6. Innovation:
By experimenting and developing solutions.

7. Ethics and Integrity:
By advocating and applying high ethical standards every day.

We strive to make medical treatment more accessible to all and support healthcare systems through cost savings. We aim to make healthcare more convenient for our customers to let them enjoy their lives. We believe that affordable medical treatment will allow savings for every member of the family. These savings will allow you to enjoy a better lifestyle.

Our Goals


• Set a new standard for quality, safety, and public trust.


• Support innovation in new medicinal infrastructure.


• Become the most cost effective and reliable medicinal provider for the region.


• Develop local pharmaceutical industries to its maximum potential.
